================================================================================
OVERAGE LOGIC (why prices are set this way)
================================================================================
  BASIC  $0.02/req  — expensive on purpose, pushes free users to upgrade
  PRO    $0.01/req  — half of BASIC
  ULTRA  $0.005/req — half of PRO
  MEGA   $0.002/req — cheapest, rewards highest-tier subscribers

  Soft limits = users are never hard-blocked, they just get billed for overages.
